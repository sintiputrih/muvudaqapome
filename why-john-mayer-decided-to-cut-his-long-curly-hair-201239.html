Why John Mayer Decided to Cut His Long, Curly Hair

John Mayer's grungy mane is long gone!

The "Shadow Days" singer, 34, debuted a sleek new 'do August 25 at The Hollywood Reporter's party to celebrate Mindy Kaling's new FOX comedy series, The Mindy Project.

The musician stopped by stylist Sally Hershberger's L.A. salon the day prior to get his hair makeover. "I was was thinking the night before that I wanted to cut his hair off, and when John came into the salon that is exactly what he wanted to do without me saying a word," Hershberger told Haute Living. "We have a flow that just works."
