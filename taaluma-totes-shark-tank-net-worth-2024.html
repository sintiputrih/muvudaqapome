Understanding Taaluma Totes’ Business Model

Taaluma Totes operates on a business model that is as unique as their products. The company sources traditional fabrics from different countries around the world and turns them into stylish tote bags. Each bag not only represents a country’s culture but also supports the local economy where the fabric was sourced. A portion of the profits is then used to fund microloans in the fabric’s country of origin, creating a cycle of sustainability and support.
