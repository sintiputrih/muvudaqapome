Kim Kardashian Reveals Whats Inside Norths (Hermes) Diaper Bag

Well, it is a spacious purse! Kim Kardashian revealed via her website that she uses a black Hermès Birkin as North West’s diaper bag. The mom of daughter Nori, 2, and son Saint West, 3 months, with husband Kanye West, penned a blog post about the essentials that she keeps stashed in the superpricy tote. (For the record, Birkins start at $11,000.)

“My diaper bag for North has a mix of the essentials and some of her favorite things — like all-natural lollipops and Minnie Mouse pajamas,” the Selfish author, 35, shared before detailing each item.

Among the necessities are Annie’s Homegrown Cheddar Bunnies snacks, raisins and organic gummy bears. The busy mom also keeps Jessica Alba’s Honest Company’s baby wipes handy as well as a hair brush to tame North’s curls.
