Who is Gabriel Soto? Gabriel Soto Biography and Wiki

Gabriel Soto is a Mexican actor and model who is a native of Mexico City, Mexico in North America. His other notable work can be seen in movies and TV shows including Vino el amor, Caer en tentación, Un refugio para el amor, Sortilegio among others you will find listed.

    Gabriel Soto Modeling

    Soto entered the modeling profession at age 18. In 1996, he was named first runner-up in the Mister World contest held in Istanbul, Turkey. A year later, the model began singing in the band Kairo replacing Eduardo Verástegui. He recorded two albums with the band including Libres and Pasiones. Afterward, Soto began participating as an actor in soap operas, scoring lead roles in most of them.
