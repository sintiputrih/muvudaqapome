Photos Of Britney Spears Ex Hollywood Life

Jason Alexander, born 1982 and not to be confused with the ‘Seinfeld’ actor, is most known as Britney Spears’ first ex-husband. The pop star and her childhood friend got hitched in a Las Vegas quickie ceremony on Jan. 3, 2004. They were wed for 55 hours before the union was annulled. The annulment said that Britney ‘lacked understanding of her actions, to the extent that she was incapable of agreeing to the marriage.’

Jason found himself in hot water come 2022, when he crashed Britney’s wedding to Sam Asghari. He tried to live stream the event and was captured scuffling with police officers on camera.
