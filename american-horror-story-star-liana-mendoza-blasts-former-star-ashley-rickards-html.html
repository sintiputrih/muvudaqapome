American Horror Story star Liana Mendoza blasts former co-star Ashley Rickards

American Horror Story actress Liana Mendoza does not want her former co-star, Ashley Rickards, to return to the hit FX show.

The 31-year-old, who plays a cop on AHS, spoke candidly to TMZ following the Pacoima Holiday Parade in Los Angeles over the weekend.

After TMZ told her that Ashley hopes to come back to the show, Liana said: 'She should have been way nicer to me on Behaving Badly, I'll say that.'

The former co-stars both appeared in the 2014 film, Behaving Badly, with Selena Gomez and Mary-Louise Parker.

Ashley, who currently stars on MTV's Awkward, played a cheerleader during season one of American Horror Story.

When asked if she thinks Ashley deserves to come back, Liana responded: 'If they don't take into account she's a b**** then yeah why not.'

Liana also told TMZ: 'She's got some fans but hasn't she switched over to the whole Disney thing or something? I don't know if that fits in anymore, but who knows.' 

Adding: 'Well you leave an Emmy award winning show--I would think that that's kind of a step back.'

While Ashley's character was killed off, she hopes to return to the show, according to Hollywoodlife.com.

Hopeful: While Ashley's character was killed off, she hopes to return to the show, according to Hollywoodlife.com

During an interview with Hollywood Life, Ashley said she's open to returning: 'Yes and I hope that this ends up on a Google Alert with Ryan [Murphy] and Brad [Falchuk] because I am dying to go back on that show.'

The 23-year-old also told the site that she's a big fan of this season: 'I love that show. This season has been so great. Lady Gaga's absolutely fantastic to watch.' 

Ashley added: 'I really don't think there's anybody who could have played that precise amount of super human, not really because she's not, and also that ever so slight vulnerability. You just get to watch this completely fascinating performance and she just nails it.'
