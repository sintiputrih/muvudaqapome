Interesting Free Agent Backstage At 1/17 AEW Dynamite

You never know who will show up in AEW next, and a free agent with a ton of potential and experience is backstage at Dynamite this week. This could result in another big debut for Tony Khan’s company.

Sean Sapp noted behind Fightful’s paywall that a very interesting name is backstage at AEW Dynamite this week. Kamille, who is a free agent, is around backstage, and that is quite intriguing all things considered.

According to the outlet, Kamille is present at the North Charleston, South Carolina episode of AEW Dynamite. Recent reports indicated that WWE was likely to extend an offer to her to join the NXT brand.

“Fightful Select has learned that top free agent Kamille is backstage at the North Charleston, South Carolina episode of AEW Dynamite. Fightful was told in recent weeks that WWE was expected to make an offer to her to join the NXT brand.“

“Those that we’ve spoken to haven’t confirmed she’s signed a deal as of yet, and believed that she was visiting friends at the show.”

Individuals Sapp reportedly spoke to have not verified her signing a deal thus far, speculating that she might be visiting friends at the event. Kamille had been a pivotal figure in the NWA Women’s division for an extended period, boasting a historic reign that stood among the longest in history.

We will have to see what happens in this situation. Obviously, a lot can happen, and she could always be around to visit or check things out. For more updates, keep checking back with us here at Ringside News.
