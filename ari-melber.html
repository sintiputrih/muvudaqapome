Ari Melber

Ari Naftali Melber is an American attorney and journalist who is the chief legal correspondent for MSNBC and host of The Beat with Ari Melber

Ari Naftali Melber, born on 31st March 1980, in Seattle, Washington to Barbara D. Melber, mother, and Daniel M. Melber, father. Melber is an American attorney and chief legal correspondent for MSNBC and host of The Beat with Ari Melber.

In his line of journalism, Ari Melber has interviewed high profiled personalities like Attorney General Eric Holder, Supreme Court Justice Stephen Breyer, Judge Richard Posner, U.S. Solicitor General Don Verrilli, Senators Ted Cruz and Rand Paul, Sheriff Joe Arpaio, and a wide range of policymakers and experts.
