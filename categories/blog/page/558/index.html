published on January 25, 2024

According to the Fifth Judicial District Attorney, Eric Christopher Gurule was sentenced to life without the possibility of parole for the murder of his uncle and former boss, Randy Flores, 55, in 2020. “I would like to thank the efforts of law enforcement and particularly the Colorado Bureau of Investigation for their quick response and willingness to lead this investigation,” said Heidi McCollum, 5th Judicial District Attorney.
