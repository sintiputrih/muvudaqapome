Matt Kalil: A Wiki Biography

Matt Kalil, born on July 6, 1989, is an American football offensive tackle who plays for the Minnesota Vikings in the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at the University of Southern California and was chosen as the fourth overall pick in the 2012 NFL Draft by the Minnesota Vikings. Following the 2012 NFL season, he was selected for the Pro Bowl.
