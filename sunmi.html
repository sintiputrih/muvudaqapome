Early Life and Childhood

She was born on May 2, 1992, and she is currently 31 years old. She is a native of Iksan, North Jeolla, South Korea, and her zodiac sign is Taurus.

Talking about her educational journey, She attended Hwangnam Elementary School, Chungdam Middle School, and Chungdam High School. She later graduated from Dongguk University majoring in musical theater.

Talking about her family, She is the eldest of three siblings and has two younger brothers. At the young age of 12, Sunmi decided to become a celebrity as this was the fastest way to make money for her.

The young lady was actually inspired by BoA when her father was admitted to the hospital due to complications of pulmonary tuberculosis.

Subsequently, she traveled to Seoul and auditioned to become a JYP trainee at the young age of 14. Sadly, only 3 months prior to her debut with the girl group Wonder Girls, Sunmi’s father passed away.

Later on, she adopted her step-father’s surname “Lee” while she was studying in university. Hence, her legal name at present is Lee Sun-mi, written as 이선미 in Korean and in Hanja as 李宣美.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, She (stylized as SUNMI), is a South Korean singer-songwriter. Internationally, She is famous as a former member of the South Korean girl group Wonder Girls.

The other original members of this group are Yeeun, Sunye, Hyuna, and Sohee. The girls debuted on February 10, 2007, with the single Irony. Prior to their debut, She was revealed as the 4th member of Wonder Girls in May 2006.

Moreover, She also participated as part of Wonder Girls in American activities. She also toured alongside the Jonas Brothers under the nickname Mimi. However, the young singer left Wonder Girls in 2010 to pursue an academic career.

This news was announced in January 2010 but the singer stayed to complete her scheduled activities till February of the same year. When she left the group, Woo Hye-rim replaced her in the group. Hyerim is a famous South Korean singer-songwriter, rapper, and actress.

Following 3 years of hiatus, She came back as a soloist. She made her solo debut with the 2013 EP called Full Moon. This EP included #2 singles on the national Gaon Digital Chart namely 24 Hours and Full Moon.

2015 Onwards

In the year 2015, She rejoined Wonder Girls for the group’s promotion activities. She was with her group until their disbandment in the year 2017.

Subsequently, the singer left JYP Entertainment and joined MAKEUS Entertainment. This company is now called ABYSS Company and she signed the contract with the company on March 14, 2017.

Under the new agency, She released the hit single Gashina on August 22, 2017. Teddy Park of The Black Label produced this song and Billboard named it the 3rd best k-pop song of 2017.

Moreover, She released her 2nd EP called Warning in 2018. The singer also produced two hit singles namely Heroine and Siren. Further, the singer also plays bass.

Besides singing, She has also made cameo appearances in various TV drama series like 2015’s The Producers, 2018’s YG Future Strategy Office, and 2020’s XX. 

The singer has also appeared in reality shows including Fashion King Korea in 2014 as a contestant; Secret Unnie in 2018; and as a cast member in Running Girls in 2020. Moreover, she also hosts Youtube’s show RReal World in 2020 with her two brothers.
