Hira Mani Biography - Age, Husband, Brothers, Dramas List

Hira Mani is a talented Pakistani actress who joined the showbiz industry despite having two kids and became successful due to her hard work and talent. Hira Mani’s actual name is Hira Salman but her husband Salman is famous as Mani, so, she is also famous as Hira Mani. She has been part of some top emotional and romantic Pakistani drama serials. Here we have Hira Mani complete Biography. So, let’s dig in to know more about your favorite actress.

Hira Mani Biography

Age & Height

She was born on 24th June 1989. She is 32 Years old now (2022). She got married to Mani at the age of 19 and joined the showbiz industry in 2012 at the age of 22.

Education

Hira Mani completed her school-level education in Karachi. After intermediate, she got married to Mani. She completed her graduation after marriage.

Family

Hira Mani’s family lives in Karachi. Her father is a financer. She is the only sister of four brothers.

Hira Mani Husband

Hira Mani is wife of Mani. The real name of Mani is Salman Saqib Sheikh. Hira and Mani’s marriage was a love marriage. Hira was a fan of Mani and loved her personality. She proposed to Mani in the first meeting. She has two kids. Her first kid Muzammil was born in 2008. Her second kid Ibrahim was born in 2012.

Career

Hira started her career at the age of 22 in 2012. She was married and has two kids but still, she worked hard and made an incredible place in fans’ hearts. Her outclass acting in the drama serial, Yakeen ka Safer, Do Bol, and Mere Pass Tum Ho made her one of the most famous actresses of her era.
