Get To Know Nicole Layog! Her Bio, Dating, Family, Height, Big Brother 24

Dramas on Big Brother are nothing new. But in season 24, the shenanigans started right from the very first week when Nicole Layog was excluded from the women-only alliance.

Let’s get to know this contestant better as this Nicole Layog Bio proceeds.

Nicole Layog On Big Brother 24

CBS Big Brother 24 hit the screen on July 14, 2022, and as always there appeared 16 contestants including Nicole Layog eying that stunning $500,000 cash prize. But chances that Nicole might be the victor seemed slim. She was already up for elimination on the first week, plus, the initial all-women grouping excluded her.

If you ask us, Nicole wasn’t asked to be a part of the women-only alliance because she was spending a lot of time hanging around the guys. However, looking at the women-only alliances’ history, it indeed always collapsed because of one or two members who run and expose the alliance to the men of the house when it came to their safety.

But then again, the intentional exclusion might have fired back on the group for Nicole also had voting power that would help the women of the house.

According to Nicole, the houseguest would perceive her as a fierce competitor. “I definitely don’t back down. And I’m very strong-willed. So I hope that that carries me through the game,” she added. Also, she was sure that everyone would like her for she was “pretty easygoing” with a “super positive attitude,” and easily build up relationships with everyone.

As for her type of alliances, she’d choose the floaters. “I want us to be able to all give in as much as we can in an alliance so that we’re all putting in the same work,” she explained.

Joining Nicole for season 24 of the reality show were houseguests — Jasmine Davis, Monte Taylor, Kyle Capener, Nicole Layog, Taylor Hale, Matt Turner, Joseph Abdin, Terrance Higgins, Michael Bruner, Indy Santos, Ameerah Jones, Paloma Aguilar, Alyssa Snider, Brittany Hoopes, Daniel Durston, and Joe “Pooch.”

Among them, Daniel and Joe won the title “Head of Household” in week 1.

Nicole Layog Family

Nicole Layog comes from a family of seven.

Her parents are Robert (a.k.a Bobby) and Rebecca Layog, 66 and 55 years of age respectively in 2022. Talking more about them, Bobby is a Bay, Laguna native who studied Dental Lab Technology at Lyons Institue. He then went on to join the U.S Army at the 3rd Infantry Division from 1977 to August 1980 and continued his career in dentistry labs.

As for Rebecca, she hails from Mariveles, Bataan. She attended Llamas Memorial Institute and went on to work at Renaissance Fort Lauderdale West.

The last we checked, Nicole’s parents were married and lived in Cooper City, FL. Surprisingly, they were only married in June 2012. Or at least this was what Rebecca’s FB mentioned.

Talking about Nicole’s siblings, she has three brothers named Clark, Doniel, and DJ, and a sister named Carmelita Layog.

Her 2-year-older sister, Carmelita is an NC-based broker/realtor who worked for Fulcrum Residential Advisors. Previously, she was employed at MTH Real Estate for almost 7 years and Center City Realty for over 7 years. (softlay.com)

Fun Fact: Nicole and Carmelita have identical voices.
