Are Simone Biles and Jonathan Owens still together?

The love story of Simone Biles and Jonathan Owens has been a source of admiration for many. Here's a detailed look at their relationship and whether they are still together.

A Match Made in Athlete Heaven

  • The couple first met via the dating app Raya in March 2020, and despite Owens initially not being aware of Biles' Olympic accomplishments, their bond grew stronger during the pandemic-induced break in Biles' training schedule.
  • They tied the knot in April 2023 after a whirlwind romance, with Biles sharing a picture of them kissing in their snow-covered backyard ahead of a game.
  • Owens made headlines after calling himself "the catch" in their relationship but later praised Biles after she was named the Associated Press' Female Athlete of the Year, demonstrating their enduring support for each other.

    • The Journey to Marriage

  • Biles and Owens announced their engagement in February 2022, and in April 2023, they officially got married in a small ceremony, as shared by Biles on Instagram.
  • Their relationship blossomed with shared moments, such as Biles supporting Owens at NFL practices and Owens joining Biles' family for weekly Sunday dinners, showing their commitment to each other's lives.

    • The Continuing Love Story

  • In a recent interview, Biles spoke about their relationship, highlighting how they are always in touch when apart and how they clicked due to their similar schedules, emphasizing the strength of their bond.
  • Despite debates over who is the better athlete, the couple's mutual admiration and support for each other's careers have been evident, indicating a harmonious partnership.

    • Simone Biles and Jonathan Owens are still together and continue to showcase their love and support for each other. Their enduring bond, from the early days of their relationship to their recent marriage, reflects a strong and lasting connection. As they navigate their respective athletic careers and personal lives, their love story remains an inspiration to many.
