Christina El Moussa Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Christina El Moussa (born July 9, 1983, in Anaheim Hills, CA) is an American TV personality and reality star best known for her role on Flip or Flop, in which she co-hosts with her now ex-husband Tarek El Moussa. She is also the co-founder of the agency, The El Moussa Group, which she runs with Tarek. She began her career early in real estate, right after graduating college. She and Tarek met when he was 21, and they began selling real estate together in Southern California.

Best Known For:
Christina is best known for starring on the hit HGTV show, Flip or Flop.

Personal Life:
Christina married Tarek El Moussa in 2009, and they have two children together — son Brayden James and daughter Taylor Reese. The couple announced their separation on Dec. 12, 2016 and also said they had been split up since May 2016. They’re currently dating other people.
