Why Did Dak Prescott And Natalie Buffett Break Up?

Oftentimes, NFL players get with other celebrities on their level of fame and status. That isn't really the case for Prescott and his latest long-term relationship.

Prescott’s 2021 NFL campaign didn't end so well. Prescott and the Cowboys lost to the San Francisco 49ers in the NFL's Wild Card round. However, Prescott's nightmare didn't end there, as he also ended things with model Natalie Buffett, Page Six announced.

The couple was believed to have broken up in March 2022, although things weren't confirmed until 2023.

Just how were things beforehand? Here's an exact timeline of how things were, and why exactly they split from each other.

The two confirmed their relationship when Buffett posted about Prescott's birthday on Instagram in 2020. Later that year, she posted a heartfelt tribute to Prescott, once again on Instagram.

They’ve also publicly celebrated the 2021 holidays by posting about each other on both Instagram and Twitter, most posts coming from Buffett's end.

Prescott and Buffett then made their official red carpet debut at the 2021 NFL Honors ceremony, where Prescott won the Comeback Player of the Year award.

Does Dak Prescott Truly Love Jadyn Jannasch?

While a lot of information has come out about Jannasch, and a decent amount of details about their relationship, the answer to this question isn't quite so obvious. Both sides seem to be fine with where things are at now, but there's more than meets the eye.

First things first - what exactly is a rebound relationship?

Rebound relationships can mean different things to different people. Typically, a rebound relationship is one that is entered into shortly after the end of a previous relationship. Yes, that fits the description of this particular saga.

The term “rebound” suggests that the person is recovering from a breakup and looking for someone new to help them move on. For that to be the case, Prescott needed to have been crazily in love with Buffett. Given the social media posts and how much PDA they had on social media and on the red carpets, that appears to be true.

Given that context, it's easy to point to Prescott and Jannasch's fling as a rebound relationship of sorts. However, it is important to note that not all relationships that begin soon after a breakup are rebound relationships. Just because those insiders close to the situation believe it is, doesn't mean the two aren't growing feelings for each other.

At this point, they've been seeing each other for at least 6 months. Rebounds don't normally last that long, at least if one goes by the average description of a rebound relationship. According to Page Six, Prescott and Jannasch have been "more active" since the holiday season, meaning things are definitely heating up.

Given the timeline, Jannasch was there by Prescott's side after his devastating loss to the 49ers. That can certainly help accelerate things if she gave him the love and support he needed, as true love and feelings grow from tough times.

Many fans dislike Patrick Mahome's wife Brittany. She's angered countless celebrities as well. However, she's been by his side during countless big losses, which has undoubtedly been a huge factor in their relationship's success. The same could be said for Gisele Bundchen, who's claimed she's "sacrificed" enough of her life to be by Tom Brady's side.

If Prescott and Jannasch are to be successful, they'll certainly need to learn from those couple's playbooks.
