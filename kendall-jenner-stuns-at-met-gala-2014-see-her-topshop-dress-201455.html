Kendall Jenner Stuns at Met Gala 2014, See Her Topshop Dress

Now that's a gown! Kendall Jenner looked flawless at the Metropolitan Museum of Art's Costume Institute Gala.

The 18-year-old model shared a snap of herself before hitting up the famed ball, looking more sophisticated than usual in a light pink Topshop floor length gown and Chopard diamond choker necklace.

Jenner kept her hair classic yet chic, wearing it down with a bit of a mess to it. 

Kim Kardashian's younger sister then paired the ensemble with Tabitha Simmons shoes and a chic bedazzled clutch.
