How did Liza Koshy get famous?

American actress, Liza Koshy became famous in 2013 when she started posting funny videos on the then-popular social media platform, Vine, under the pseudonym “Lizza.” She usually posted short comic videos. In 2017, when Vine closed down, she has over 7 million followers. She took her talent to YouTube to continue entertaining her fans in 2016. Currently, she has managed to gather 17 million subscribers and two YouTube channels and has a total of over 3 billion views.

She made her debut appearance in a movie as Ada Walker in a comedy horror movie titled ‘Boo! A Madea Halloween,’ written and directed by Tyler Perry. In 2017, she played The Explorer in the YouTube Premium series ‘Escape the Night.’ She also starred as Violet Adams in the Hulu drama series Freakish from 2016 to 2017.

From 2018 to 2020, she produced and starred in the YouTube Premium series ‘Liza on Demand.’ In 2019, she was included in the Forbes 30 under 30 Hollywood & Entertainment list and the Time Magazine’s List of the 25 Most Influential People on the Internet.
