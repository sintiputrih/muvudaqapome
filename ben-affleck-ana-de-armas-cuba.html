Ben Affleck and Ana de Armas Were Reportedly Seen &quot;Making Out&quot; in Cuba

Ana de Armas is quickly becoming Hollywood’s most in-demand leading lady — she has five movies out this year alone — but when it comes to flashy romances, Armas has kept a fairly low profile … until now, perhaps?

Armas plays opposite Ben Affleck in thriller Deep Water, and it seems their dynamic plays quite well off-screen as well. Following shooting in New Orleans, which concluded in February, the co-stars took a trip together to Armas’s native Cuba.

The actor duo took several photos with fans and restaurant employees during their getaway.

Though neither actor’s reps have returned InStyle’s request for comment, a source told People that Armas and Affleck are “definitely dating.”

According to the report, before they left Cuba (via private jet) on Saturday night, the pair was spotted “making out” in the airport.

Affleck was previously married to actress Jennifer Garner, with whom he shares three children. They split after nearly a decade of marriage in 2015. Armas has also been married — to Spanish actor Marc Clotet. After about two years of marriage, Armas and Clotet split in 2013.

During a recent interview with Diane Sawyer, Affleck said he'd "love to have a relationship that is deeply meaningful and one to which I could be deeply committed," and expanded that in five years he’d like to be in a “healthy, stable, loving, committed relationship.”
