Jason DeRulo Gifts 10,000 Roses to Jordin Sparks For Valentine's Day

Looks like Kanye West needs to step up his game! After Kim Kardashian shared a photo of her 1,000 red roses Valentine’s Day surprise, R&B artist Jason DeRulo took it a step further with his gift to longtime girlfriend Jordin Sparks — surprising the former American Idol winner, 24, with 10,000 orange roses. 

“Valentine’s Day I got Jordin 10,000 roses. Her favorite color is orange. So they were all orange,” DeRulo, 24, told Us Weekly at the Cartoon Network 4th Annual Hall of Game Awards on Feb. 15. “Passion Roses took care of me. I knew it was outrageous, but they made it happen for me.”

Knowing the clean up for 10,000 roses would be extreme, the “Talk Dirty” crooner rented a suite at the Redbury Hotel to present his surprise. Once Sparks saw her present, the couple ended up gifting the remaining flowers to the hotel staff. 

“She was absolutely overwhelmed and shocked,” DeRulo continued. “She was like, ‘I have never seen this many flowers in one place before.’ I was like, ‘Me neither, actually.'”

Sparks clearly loved her surprise, taking to Instagram to show them off.

And that wasn’t the end of the romantic day. After doling out the flowers, Sparks and DeRulo noshed on lobster pizza from Berri’s Pizza Cafe and sipped moscato.
