Justin Hartley is a prominent American actor, producer, and director. Justin Hartley is famous for appearing in TV shows and films including ‘Passions’, ‘Red Canyon‘, ‘The Hunt, and ‘This Is Us’ among others.

Likewise, his full birth name is Justin Scott Hartley. At the time of his birth, Justin’s family was residing in Knoxville, Illinois. He then grew up in the Chicago suburb of Orland Park. Professional Life

Professionally, Justin Hartley is a popular actor. The actor is famous for his TV roles including the role of Fox Crane on Passions, Oliver Queen/Green Arrow on Smallville, and Adam Newman on The Young and the Restless. The latter earned Justin an Emmy nomination.

Likewise, Justin appeared as the character Patrick Osbourne in season 3 of the TV series Revenge. Since 2016, he has starred as Kevin Pearson in This Is Us, for which he was nominated for the Critics’ Choice Television Award for Best Supporting Actor in a Drama Series.

In the year 2006, Justin played the starring role of the character Aquaman in the TV pilot Aquaman (or Mercy Reef). The actor then went on to appear in a seven-episode run as billionaire Oliver Queen on the series Smallville. Similarly, in the year 2008, Justin returned to Smallville as a regular cast member. This was possible following the departures of the cast members Kristin Kreuk, Michael Rosenbaum, Laura Vandervoort, and John Glover.

Justin is also a writer and director who co-wrote the 2010 episode “Sacrifice” and also directed the 2011 episode “Dominion”. Again in 2008, Justin played the role of the character Tom in the film Red Canyon. The Badlands of Utah filmed this project.
