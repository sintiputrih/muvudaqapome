Jasmine Tookes shines in sultry campaign for Logan Hollowell's first engagement collection

Recently-engaged Jasmine Tookes strips down for jewelry designer Logan Hollowell’s first bridal and engagement collection campaign.

The 30-year-old puts her enviable figure on display while draped in sparkly jewels made with conflict-free diamonds and 100% recycled 14k or 18k gold.

The Logan Hollowell Eternal Collection is launching with 20 initial styles that tie together tradition with modern day ideals.

After dating Snapchat’s Juan David Borrero for four years, Juan proposed to Jasmine on September 21. 

With nuptials on the brain, the model brought an effortless elegance and an unwavering sense of confidence and strength to the images, she was an obvious choice to star in the campaign.

Striking sultry pose after sultry pose, Jasmine drips in Logan Hollowell necklaces and bracelets and her digits are decorated with multiple diamond rings.

‘I wanted to create a collection of engagement rings for every type of bride,’ shared the LA-based designer\

While weddings have almost always been wrapped in tradition, today’s bride is rejecting societal norms, and that includes her ring.  

‘I wanted to create a collection of engagement rings for every type of bride,’ shared the LA-based designer.

‘I was inspired by the different archetypes that exist within a woman. Women are like diamonds, with many facets and no two the same.  

‘Once actualized through our own Alchemy, we become unbreakable – just like a diamond. 

‘Throughout the design process I worked to join both classic and modern elements to create something that would instantly become timeless. One of my greatest joys is working one-on-one with clients to create their own custom dream ring.’

 ‘I wanted to create a collection of engagement rings for every type of bride,’ shared the LA-based designer’

The collection features a variety of premium diamond cuts including Oval, Round, Asscher, Emerald, Pear and Old Mine.

Setting styles range from a minimalistic solitaire to ornate pavé three-row, while others with organic-style settings pay homage to the bestselling Queen Collection. 

All of the rings from the collection and design studio are made from 100% recycled gold, conflict-free diamonds and ethically sourced gemstones. 

Prices start at $5,500 .

In addition to the collection, the brand will launch the Logan Hollowell Ring Design Studio where a bespoke ring can be created from start to finish. 

Customers can send their design inspiration to schedule a personal design consultation. They will be able to select their stone shape, center stone type, setting style, gold color, and customized band. 

‘With our new Ring Design Studio, the sky is truly the limit on what you can design, and we are here to bring it to life for you,’ said Logan.
