Career

Aphex Twin is a British singer, composer, and DJ best known by his stage name. He is best known for his idiosyncratic work in electronic genres such as techno and ambient music in the 1990s, as well as his association with the electronic subgenre known as intelligent dance music (IDM), which he has rejected. James was dubbed “the most creative and powerful figure in contemporary electronic music” by Guardian journalist Paul Lester in 2001.
