Jennie Kim is a South Korean K-pop star who is the vocalist of the famous South Korean girl band, Black Pink. She is a style diva and a treasured YG Entertainment artist. Jennie has also appeared in G-Dragon’s music video “That XX”. She has a huge fan base and is often referred to as ‘The YG Princess’.

Boyfriend / Spouse

Like the other members of the Black Pink band, Jennie is also prohibited from dating. She revealed that she would prefer someone who is a hard worker.

Her name was linked with –

  • RM (2017) – BTS band member RM was rumored to be dating with Jennie in 2017. They were spotted leaving a restaurant, which led to the rumors that they were out on a date. But, the duo was never seen together publicly after that day.
  • Kim Jong-in (2018-2019) – Exo’s Kai or Kim Jong-in allegedly started dating Jennie in October 2018. They had spent time together during Paris Fashion Week. They were also seen together on November 25, 2018, as per pictures released by Dispatch. Finally, in January 2019, the dating news was confirmed by SM Entertainment who manages Kai’s band Exo. Few days after that, on January 25, 2019, the news of Kim Jong-in and Jennie’s separation was confirmed.
  • G-Dragon (2021) – In February 2021, it was reported that Jennie was dating the Big Bang member and solo artist, G-Dragon.
  • V (2022-Present) – In 2022, Jennie was rumored to be dating BTS‘ V. In May 2023, the couple again ignited the romance rumors after they were spotted holding hands during a late-night walk in France.

    Jennie Kim Facts

  • She is fluent in Korean, Japanese, and English.
  • Jennie’s mother wanted her to be a teacher or a lawyer.
  • Jennie is a single child.
  • She has 2 pet dogs, Kai and Kuma.
  • She joined YG Entertainment in 2010 when she was only 14 and has trained with them for more than 6 years.
  • Jennie was the preferred lead for the band, Black Pink, but somehow the band ended up without a lead due to the ages of its members.
  • She got featured in an MBC documentary “English, Must Change to Survive” at the age of 10.
  • She is considered to be a YG trump card and is often regarded as ‘The YG Princess’.
  • In 2012, Jennie appeared in the music video of the song “That XX” by G-Dragon.
  • Her fluency in English is credited to her studying in New Zealand.
  • She is the group’s style icon.
  • Her bandmates regard her as the best cook amongst them.
  • She has modeled with other Black Pink members for magazines like Nylon Japan, South Korea’s 1st Look, Elle Korea, and Singapore’s Teenage.
  • Her friends often call her ‘Human Gucci’ because Jennie often wears clothes from the upscale fashion brand Gucci.
  • In November 2018, Jennie released her debut single titled Solo. The song debuted at No. 1 on the Gaon Music Chart. It was certified ‘platinum’ by the KMCA and also won the “Song of the Year” award at the Gaon Chart Music Awards.
  • In April 2019, she became the first Korean solo artist to perform at Coachella.
  • In April 2020, Jennie teamed up with the South Korean luxury eyewear brand, Gentle Monster to create a collection called Jentle Home.
  • As of December 2020, she was the 2nd most followed k-pop idol on her personal Instagram with over 37 million followers.
  • In May 2023, Jennie made her Met Gala debut. For the event, she wore an ivory duchesse satin bustier dress embellished with a white camélia and a black ribbon.
  • In October 2023, Jennie released a new single titled You & Me.
  • In November 2023, Jennie and other members of Blackpink were named “Honorary Members of the Order of the British Empire” for their work as COP26 advocates during the COP26 Summit in Glasgow in 2021.
  • In December 2023, Jennie confirmed that she had launched her own agency called OA (ODD ATELIER).
  • In December 2023, it was revealed that Jennie and other members of BLACKPINK had renewed their contracts with YG Entertainment for group activities. However, the members had not renewed their individual contracts with the agency for solo activities.
