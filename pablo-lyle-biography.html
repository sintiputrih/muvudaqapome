Who is Pablo Lyle?

Pablo Lyle is a Mexican actor. He is widely recognized for his roles in La Sombra del Pasado and Por Siempre mi Amor.

Pablo Lyle: Age, Parents, Education, Ethnicity

He was born on November 18, 1986, in Mazatlán, Sinaloa, México. His birth name is Pablo Daniel Lyle López. He has not disclosed any information regarding his parents, siblings, and childhood.

Pablo holds American citizenship but his ethnicity is Mexican.

He attended a Catholic school and graduated from there. After graduation, he began his career as a model.

Pablo Lyle: Professional Life and Career

Pablo Lyle started his career as a model and he began his acting career in the year 2005. He made his debut in ‘Verano de Amor’ in 2009 as Baldomero Perea Olmos.

After that, he made his appernaces in ‘Mujeres asesinas’ (2010), ‘Una familia con suerte’ (2011), ‘Cachito de cielo’ (2012), ‘Como dice el dicho’ (2013), and as Esteban in ‘Por siempre mi amor’ (2013-2014).

Pablo Lyle has made his breakthrough in ‘La Sombra del Pasado’ as Cristóbal Mendoza Rivero 2014, the lead character of the series, and appeared in the series for a year.

His other notable appernaces include in ‘Corazón que miente’ (2016), ‘Mi adorable maldición’ (2017) and also appeared as a guest in ‘La rosa de Guadalupe’ (2015), ‘Érase una vez’ (2017), ‘Conan’ (2017).
