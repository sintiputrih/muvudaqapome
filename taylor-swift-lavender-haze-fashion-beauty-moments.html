Lavender Haze: Taylor Swifts best fashion and beauty moments from her new music video - shop now

Just when we thought Miley Cyrus and Flowers had given us the ‘independent woman’ encouragement we all need from time-to-time, Taylor Swift comes along and releases the video to her song Lavender Haze, reminding everybody what it's like to be in the honeymoon phase of a relationship. Thanks a lot, Taylor. 

(FYI, ‘In the lavendar haze' is a common phrase from the 1950s meaning to be deeply in love with someone or still in the honeymoon stage.)
