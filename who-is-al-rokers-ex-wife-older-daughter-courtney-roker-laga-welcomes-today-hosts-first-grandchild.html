Who is Al Roker's ex-wife? Older daughter Courtney Roker Laga welcomes 'Today' host's first grandchi

LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA: Al Roker, the beloved weather presenter and TV personality, recently shared the joyous news of the birth of his daughter Courtney Roker Laga's first child, making him a proud grandfather for the first time.

The 68-year-old 'Today' host is married to Deborah Roberts, his second wife, while his daughter Courtney is from his first marriage to Alice Bell. Al shares two more children, Leila and Nick, with his current wife, Deborah. Following the arrival of his granddaughter, Al has been relishing precious family time and enjoying the holiday moments during his break from the show. He took to Instagram to share his happiness, writing, "And then this little girl came into my life. Welcome to the world, Sky Clara Laga. In love all over again. Thank you, Courtney and Wes for such a perfect blessing. You baby is beautiful. Already planning the ways to spoil her and then hand her back."

Who is Al Roker's current wife, Deborah Roberts?

Deborah Roberts, born on September 20, 1960, in Perry, Georgia, is an accomplished television journalist who currently works as the Senior National Affairs Correspondent for ABC News. She has a strong presence on social media, with 246k followers on her Instagram page and 52k followers on Twitter. In addition to her journalistic career, Deborah is also the author of the book, 'Lessons Learned and Cherished: The Teacher Who Changed My Life', published on May 2. Moreover, she co-wrote the book 'Been There, Done That' with her husband Al Roker, and Laura Morton in 2016, sharing their experiences and insights.
