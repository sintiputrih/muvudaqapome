Where is Muenster cheese from?

Share: Muenster is one of our country’s first semi-soft cheeses, and it originated in ancient monastic Alsace, France. It made its way to Wisconsin’s no-shortcuts, no-nonsense cheese-makers in the late 1800s. In Europe, muenster is traditionally a soft, creamy white, washed-rind cheese.
