Iggy Azalea and French Montana Are Dating: All the Details

New couple alert! Iggy Azalea and French Montana are dating, a source confirms to Us Weekly.

The “Black Widow” singer, 26, and the “Lockjaw” rapper, 31, “are very much a couple,” the insider tells Us. “They’ve been all over each other.”

Azalea and Montana have been packing on the PDA ever since they jetted off to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico, via a private jet on Saturday, August 27.

As previously reported by Us, the two were seen being flirty and playful together on the plane on Saturday. In an Instagram video posted by Baller Alert, Montana rests his head on Azalea’s booty.

In another Instagram photo posted by Montana on Sunday, August 28, the duo soak up the sun on the bow of a boat. Azalea shows off her toned physique in a striped bikini. 

Azalea first ignited rumors of a fling with Montana after they were spotted getting cozy in Las Vegas following one of Jennifer Lopez’s concerts this past July.
