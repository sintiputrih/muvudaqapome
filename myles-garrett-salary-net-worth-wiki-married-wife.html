Concerning the brawl, his high school coach, Bob Wagner, mentioned that he had never expected such actions from Myles. Also, Garrett admitted his unwanted effects over the Steelers quarterback. He stated, 

"Last night, I made a terrible mistake. I lost my cool, and what I did was selfish and unacceptable."

Family Of Sportsperson

Myles was born to his parents, Audrey and Lawrence Garrett. His mother, Audrey, was an All-American track star at Hampton University, whereas his father referred him to “an obedient kid,” who “never had a rebellious stage.”

Myles Garrett grew up with his half-brother, Sean Williams, who is from his mother's side. A former professional basketball player, Sean, previously played NBA for Boston College. Later, in 2007, New Jersey Nets drafted him as the 17th pick in the 2007 NBA Draft. Sean played his professional NBA career for five years from 2007 to 2012. 

Myles' sister, Brea Garrett, is a track and field athlete at Texas A&M, who won the 2014 NCAA title in the 20-pound weight throw.
