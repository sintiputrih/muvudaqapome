WWE House Show Results (2/3): Birmingham, Alabama

WWE Live Event results on 2/3/13 in Birmingham, Alabama:

Ryder vs. Conner O'Brien

Ryder wins via pinfall after a rough rider from second rope.

Tensai vs. Alex Riley

Vickie comes out and tries to make it a dance off, but Riley attacks Tensai from behind.

Tensai wins via pinfall

Riley grabs mic, claims his shoe was untied and demand a rematch. Bell rings and Tensai still wins.

Kaitlyn vs. Tamina
Women's Championship Match

Kaitlyn wins via pinfall to retain

Team Hell No vs The Shield (Reigns and Rollins) Tag Team Title Match

Team Hell No win via DQ when Ambrose hits Kane with a chair.

Jack Swagger vs. Justin Gabriel

Swagger wins via submission with the ankle lock

Antonio Cesaro vs. The Miz
No DQ match for the US Championship

Cesaro wins via pinfall after a low blow while Miz was going for skull crushing finale. Kendo sticks, chairs and a table were all used. Solid match for a house show.

John Cena and Ryback vs. CM Punk and Dolph Ziggler

Cena pins Ziggler for the pin.

Justin Roberts said they would be seeing us again soon, but no confirmed return date was announced.
