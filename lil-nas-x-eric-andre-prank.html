Lil Nas X Gets Pranked By Eric Andr In Bizarre Show Teaser

Lil Nas X has helped comedian Eric André celebrate the season six premiere of his eponymous show — even though he found himself unwittingly at the center of a bizarre prank.

In the teaser video for the upcoming season premiere of The Eric André Show — which dropped on Monday (May 22) — the “Old Town Road” performer comes out swinging, asking the comedian provocative questions like “do you believe in the Illuminati?”

But by the end of the clip, André turns the tables on Lil Nas X, eventually concluding by asking questions with drool dripping out of his mouth.

“What the fuck is wrong with you?” he asked André.

André told Rolling Stone that despite Lil Nas X’s reputation of being a professional troll, he was deeply affected by the way things went.

“It was great because he’s very sensitive, so he was easily affected by every prank we threw at him. He’s kind of the ideal guest,” he said.

The Eric André Show premieres on Cartoon Network’s adult swim on June 4.
