Curly Head Monty - Age, Family, Bio

Multi-talented dancer, aspiring model/actor, and social media influencer who has earned over 1.8 million followers on Instagram by primarily posting short dance videos to his curlyheadmonty account. He has been featured on several "Top 10 Best Dance Videos" on YouTube. 

Before Fame

He started working on various songs, such as "Pipe It Down," at a young age. 

Trivia

He has a self-titled YouTube channel where he has posted comedic videos ranging from pranks to challenges. He also posts his dance videos to his popular curlyheadymonty TikTok account which has over 1.7 million fans. 

Family Life

He has three older brothers and two sisters, Lyric Thomas and Daiiibabyyy. His mom manages his account. He and his ex-girlfriend Katie launched a collaborative Instagram account called Katie & Monty. 

Associated With

He posted a TikTok video that was inspired by iLoveMemphis and Ayo & Teo.
