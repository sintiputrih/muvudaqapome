Albert Joseph Manzo IV Wiki Biography

Albie Manzo was born on the 27th May 1986, in Franklin Lakes, New Jersey USA, and is a television personality and business person, best known for his role in Bravo’s reality TV show “The Real Housewives of New Jersey” (2009- ), and in its spinoff called “Manzo’d with Children” (2014- ). Manzo is also a successful entrepreneur, one of the co-founders of “The New Star Group” in 2011. Being an ambitious business person has significantly increased his net worth. He started his career on television in 2013.

Albert Joseph Manzo IV is the oldest child of Caroline Manzo, a reality television star herself, and Albert Manzo, who also occasionally appeared on TV. Albie has a sister Lauren, and brother Christopher, with whom he’s working for the “New Star Group”. He went to Ramapo High School and Fordham University before enrolling at the Seton Hall Law School, but dropped out in the first year due to low grades and learning disability. Manzo wanted to try again, but had to wait two more years before applying for the school. He did apply for other law schools, however, his school dreams vanished after numerous refused applications. Albie thinks that the actual reason for rejections is his role in the reality show “The Real Housewives of New Jersey”.

Manzo became nationally known when he appeared in the Bravo reality TV show “The Real Housewives of New Jersey” in 2010, along with his mother Caroline who is a regular cast member, and his father and siblings. The series is focused on five housewives living in New Jersey; it first aired on the 12th May 2009, and is currently in its eighth season. Albie also appeared in the talk-show “Big Morning Buzz Live” in 2013, a one-hour morning show on VH1 hosted by Carrie Keagan. Since 2014, Albie is part of another of Bravo’s reality shows called “Manzo’d with Children”, and is starring in it with the rest of his family. The story is following the lives of the five-member family and their everyday situations, of course adding to his net worth.

Albie Manzo is a very ambitious young business person, and he came up with an idea to start his own company, the “New Star Group” in 2011. The company represents popular beverages such as tribute tequila, BLK health water, and Levendi wine, among others. Their distribution covers major stores like Whole Foods, Target, Sprouts and Wegmans. Manzo is also an associate in the Hospitality Group that owns a restaurant in Hoboken, New Jersey, which recently expanded to a new location at Meadowlands Racing. Albie’s entrepreneurial skills and smart investments have helped him increase his wealth, however, Albie and his brother Christopher are facing a lawsuit from a Canadian company that claims they stole the drink’s concept, related to their Blackwater or BLK product.

When it comes to speak about his personal life, Albie Manzo dated New York Jets cheerleader Lindsey Andrews from 2011 to 2013, and is currently dating Brittany Parks, who has appeared with Albie in “Manzo’d with Children” – rumour has it that he is ‘off the market’.
