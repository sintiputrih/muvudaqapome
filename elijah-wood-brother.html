Elijah Wood Brother: Who Is Zack Wood?

Elijah Wood Brother: Who Is Zack Wood?: Elijah Wood, officially known as Elijah Jordan Wood is an American actor and producer.

He developed a passion for movies at a younger age and made his film debut with a small part in Back to the Future Part II.

Wood has since been consistent throughout his career, and has become one of the most sought-after American actors.

His film credits include; Avalon, Paradise, Radio Flyer, Forever Young, The Adventures of Huck Finn, The Good Son, North, The War, Flipper, The Ice Storm, Deep Impact, and The Faculty.

Wood has appeared in several films but is best known for his portrayal of Frodo Baggins in The Lord of the Rings film trilogy and The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey.

Following the success of The Lord of the Rings, Wood has starred in a number of films including; Spy Kids 3D: Game Over, Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind, Sin City, Green Street, and Everything Is Illuminated.

He has also appeared in the films; Bobby, Celeste and Jesse Forever, and I Don’t Feel at Home in This World Anymore. His voice roles include; Mumble, Spyro the Dragon, Uprising, and Over the Garden Wall.

Wood played Ryan Newman on Wilfred for which he received a Satellite Award nomination for Best Actor, and Todd Brotzman in the BBC America series Dirk Gently’s Holistic Detective Agency.

Wood was an active supporter of Bernie Sanders during the 2016 presidential election. He’s also a disc jockey and has toured globally with his friend Zach Cowie as the duo Wooden Wisdom.
