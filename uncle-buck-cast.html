Cast Of Uncle Buck: List Of Actors And Characters They Portray In This 1990s Comedy

Uncle Buck is a comedy-drama film from 1989. The children of the Russel family are the focus of the film. When Bob had a heart attack and his brother Buck is summoned to his home to care for his children, the story is loaded with comedy and drama. John Hughes directed and wrote the picture. Uncle Buck characters are all crafted in such a way that they will undoubtedly make you laugh, and the cast of Uncle Buck includes some fantastic actors who have now grown up.
