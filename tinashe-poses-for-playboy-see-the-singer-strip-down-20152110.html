Tinashe Poses for Playboy: See the Singer Strip Down

Tinashe is showing off her killer body in a sultry spread for the November issue of Playboy.

The “2 On” singer, 22, stripped down to her underwear for the adult magazine, which recently announced that it will no longer publish its signature nude photographs.

“Sexuality has always been part of who I am as an artist — emphasis on part,” Tinashe said. “I’m an all-around entertainer, my own creative force, and I’m bringing something fresh to the table.”

Tinashe recently debuted “Player,” the first single from her upcoming sophomore album, Joyride. The song, which features Chris Brown, followed the release of a Young Thug-assisted buzz track titled “Party Favors.”

The R&B songstress is also featured alongside Kendall Jenner in the latest issue of Vogue.
