Michael Stuhlbarg Net Worth

Michael Stuhlbarg is an American actor born on July 5, 1968 in Long Beach, CA. He is best known for his Golden Globe Award-nominated role in the 2009 film A Serious Man and his Drama Desk Award-winning performance in the 2005 play The Pillowman. He also starred in the Marvel film Doctor Strange.

About

He became recognized for his Golden Globe Award-nominated role in the 2009 film A Serious Man. As a stage actor, he earned a Drama Desk Award for Outstanding Featured Actor in a Play for his role in the 2005 play The Pillowman. He would star in the Marvel film Doctor Strange.

Before Fame

He began his acting career in the theater, where he was recognized for his award-winning role in a production of Old Wicked Songs.
