Katie Holmes Splits From Fashion Line Partnership With Jeanne Yang

Holmes & Yang might now be Holmes or Yang. Actress Katie Holmes has ended her five-year fashion line partnership with her onetime stylist Jeanne Yang. 

A source tells Page Six that the duo split due to "interpersonal conflict" after reports came out that Yang had leaked information about Holmes, 35, and ex-husband Tom Cruise following their divorce. 

The fashion line's social media pages have been quiet for more than a month, and Holmes' rep confirmed that "The Holmes & Yang partnership has ended."

According to Page Six, the high-end brand will no longer be sold at Barneys New York. Stars like Taylor Swift, Olivia Wilde, and Heidi Klum have all rocked Holmes & Yang apparel, and breakout star Lupita Nyong'o wore a cream colored maxi skirt from the brand on her Hollywood Reporter cover.
