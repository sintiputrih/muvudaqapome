Iyanna Mayweather Floyd Mayweathers Daughter Biography, Age, Mother

While some see her as an artist, others see her as a potential boxing champion. But whatever career Iyanna Mayweather chooses, she has already managed to attract a huge following on social media. It’s all thanks to his expensive and enviable lifestyle, largely funded by his father, American professional boxer Floyd Mayweather Jr.

Iyanna is known for splitting her time between working on her music and working out with her dad in the gym. She and her father, who retired with an undefeated record, are said to have a very close bond which has caused fans to call him their favorite child. Read on to learn more about Mayweather’s daughter and her family.

Biography of Iyanna Mayweather (Age)

Iyanna Mayweather was born on May 20, 2000, to Floyd Mayweather Jr. and Melissa Bim. The professional boxer has four children in all and Iyanna is his only child with his mother, who was his girlfriend once. Among his siblings are half-sister Jirah and half-brothers Koraun and Zion, from his father’s relationship with Josie Harris. She has a half-brother, Devion, from her mother’s former relationship.

Like all other normal children, Iyanna attended high school, after which she went to a higher institution, but the exact names of the schools she attended are not available. The only way the public got to know a little about her upbringing was when she posted pictures of her graduation on social media.

For the public, Iyanna Mayweather has made no significant move towards any career path. This may be the reason for the mixed reviews she received for her song in 2018. In October 2018, a music video titled Oh Alright Remix was released on YouTube. But a critical analysis of the views revealed that his striking style was central to his concerns. Iyanna has a YouTube channel and tries hard to keep it active.

But as the daughter of one of the most talented boxers of all time, she has achieved huge success on social media. With hundreds of thousands of people following her on Instagram and Twitter, Iyanna is nowhere below average in terms of social media presence. As for her popularity, Iyanna caught the public eye with lavish birthday parties sponsored by her father.

It doesn’t end there, her dad is known to spoil his daughter by giving her the most expensive things. On her 16th birthday, she invited musical artists such as Drake and Future to perform at her party and also received many gifts. This may seem like the highest of them all, but you will be amazed to know that on her 18th birthday, Iyanna Mayweather was gifted with an 18-carat “Canary PRISTINE” ring worth $5 million. Everything was glitzy at the birthday party celebrated by a host of celebrities.

All of these gifts made people wonder if she was her father’s favorite child. Another guess is that the boxer is probably trying to make up for his infamous relationship with Iyanna’s mother.

Who is his mother?

As we mentioned earlier, Iyanna Mayweather is Floyd’s only child with his one time girlfriend, Melissa Brim. Brim is said to have first met the boxer when she was a teenager. By the time they started dating, Melissa already had a son, Devion Cromwell, from a previous relationship, but that was never a problem for Floyd, who even reportedly adopted the child out of love for his mother. . But things quickly soured between them after Melissa got pregnant with Iyanna. According to some sources, they had a falling out at the time and later separated.

After Iyanna was born, the pair came back together again, Floyd did his best to catch up with the past. He helped her open a boutique, Devanna Love Boutique, named after her children. In 2012, the once beloved couple broke up again. It was a lousy episode with allegations of physical abuse against Melissa by Mayweather. Floyd got in trouble with the law and was placed under house arrest for a few days.
