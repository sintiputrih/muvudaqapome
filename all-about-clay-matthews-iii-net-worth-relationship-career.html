All About Clay Matthews III | Net Worth, Relationship, Career

Clay Matthews III is a National Football League free-agency outside linebacker. He was previously with the Green Bay Packers and the Los Angeles Rams. In the 2009 NFL Draft, he was expected to be a first-round choice.

After trading up, the Green Bay Packers chose him in the first round (26th overall). On March 19, 2019, he signed a two-year contract with the Los Angeles Rams and was released on March 19, 2020. During the 2020 season, he did not play for any team.
