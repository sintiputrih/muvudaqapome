Release information

This tag was given away to patrons at participating Namco amusement arcades who entered a lottery associated with the Piplup Mogumogu Rush! Campaign. Service tickets could be redeemed on the Namco Point app for every ¥300 spent playing eligible crane games. Two service tickets could then be used to enter the lottery, which offered Piplup-themed cups, coasters, and stickers to winners. A single service ticket could also be used to generate an in-app coupon, which together could be shown to staff in exchange for a promotional Piplup TCG card. The promotion ran between September 21 and October 31, 2021.[1]
