Amanda Peet Pregnant, Expecting Third Child: Debuts Bump at Emmys 2014

Peet’s pregnancy: Part III! Actress Amanda Peet is expecting her third child with her husband David Benioff. The star, 42, revealed her (rather large!) baby bump and confirmed her happy pregnancy news to Us Weekly on the red carpet of the 2014 Emmy Awards in L.A.

The mother of two, wearing a flowy soft printed dress by Giambattista Valli on the red carpet, lovingly pointed to her burgeoning bump on Monday, Aug. 25. Her husband, 43, the showrunner of HBO’s Game of Thrones, beamed beside her as they shared their expectancy news.

Benioff and Peet are already parents to two daughters, Frances, 7, and Molly, 4.

“They’re pretty excited,” Peet told Us of her kids. “The older one is pretty excited, the middle one is not so sure.”

Peet told Us Weekly in June 2010 that she was “too old” to have another baby — but it turns out that age is but a number. “I’m really old,” Peet told Us four years ago. Her husband, though, wanted more kids. “[He] was like, we have to wait three years,” she told Us. “And I was like, we don’t have three years! We’ll be too old at that point.”

The star laughed as she described her physical condition in her third pregnancy. “It’s okay! It’s okay!” Peet said on the Emmys red carpet. “42 and pregnant is quite something,” she added with a laugh. “I’ll be taking these heels off in a minute.”
