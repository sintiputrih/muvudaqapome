Neil Byrne was born in Ireland on November 16, 1977. Irish folk singer who first rose to fame as a member of the legendary singing group Celtic Thunder. He makes up one member of Celtic Thunder alongside Glee’s Damian McGinty. It was after watching his father’s band perform at a wedding at the age of six that he fell in love with music.
