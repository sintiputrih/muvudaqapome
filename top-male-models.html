The information and details has been compiled from trusted sites on the web, including Marie Clarie and Wikipedia.

18. Oliver Cheshire

Oliver Cheshire is a British model who took on the fashion world with Select Model Management at the tender age of 15.

The 33-year-old model has since then walked and campaigned for nearly every major designer from Calvin Klein, Versace, Dolce & Gabbana, to Marks and Spencer.

A pure fashion legend, Cheshire is a man with irresistible charm, chiseled features, a well-toned physique, and an immaculate sense of style and catwalk.

Further, he has fronted the covers of GQ and Vogue as one of the sexiest men, Top Best Dressed British Men, and Top Male Model frequently.

17. Luka Sabbat

Coming to this spot is New York’s stunning dread-lock hunky, Luka Sabbat.

The tempting model began his modeling venture with Re: Quest Model Management at the age of 15.

His modeling creds include working with the likes of Calvin Klein, Hugo Boss, and Dolce & Gabbana.

Sabbat has also appeared in ABC’s popular sitcoms, Black-ish and Grown-ish.

One of the world’s top male models, Sabbat, is rumored to have had a fling with Kourtney Kardashian in the past.

16. Evandro Soldati

The emerald eyes hottie Evandro Soldati is one of the top male models in the world.

Soldati landed in the fashion world with the likes of his mother via a Ford contest that took place in his native Brazil.

The model has walked the runways for Louis Vuitton, Valentino, and Dolce & Gabbana.

Giorgi Armani made him its lead male model for the 2007 and 2008 ads.

According to Forbes, Soldati is one of the most successful male models of all time.

15. Isaac Carew

The British model, Isaac Carew, has divine charm and is full of talents.

He has graced the runway shows and campaigns for Valentino, Hermes, Zara, and many more.

Gordon Ramsay Group’s certified chef, Carew, also has his own YouTube channel named “The Dirty Dishes,” where he shares his love for cooking and his crafty and delightful tips and recipes for his viewers.

Besides, he is also the author of the same-titled cookbook “The Dirty Dishes.”

Carew also dated the pop singer Dua Lipa from 2014 to 2017.

14. Adam Senn

Holding the 14th spot is a dashing French-born American model, Adam Senn.

Senn has walked on the runway and starred in editorials, and campaigned for designers like Gucci, Versace, Tommy Hilfiger, Versace, Dolce & Gabbana.

Aside from modeling, he is an actor who appeared in the coming-of-age drama Magic City Memoirs (2011).

His most beloved work is on VH1’s popular series “Hit the Floor,” where he played the role of Zero, a bisexual basketball player.

Further, he also co-owns an Italian restaurant Bocca di Bacco in his hometown New York.

13. Johannes Huebl

The 45 years old German hunk Johannes Huebl is one of the world’s most successful male models.

He has modeled for nearly every luxury designer, from Hugo Boss and Ralph Lauren to Donna Karen.

The sensual model has also graced the covers of Harper’s Bazaar Mexico, Elle, Vanity Fair, and the list goes on.

12. Noah Mills

Handsome hunk Noah Mills is one of the most prominent names in the modeling industry.

The Canadian stunner Mills’s breakthrough began when he made his runway debut for Gucci and Saint Lauren in 2004.

Since then, he has been the highly sought-after face of countless luxury lines, including Versace, Michael Kors, and Dolce & Gabbana.

Aside from modeling, Mills is also noted for his performance in the popular comedy film, Sex and the City 2.

Besides, you can also spot the apple of your eye in Taylor’s Swift “We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together” music video.

11. Mathias Lauridsen

Mathias Lauridsen is one of the top male models in the world, straight from Denmark.

His modeling debut began in 2004 when he walked the runway for the spring collections of Paris’s Miu Miu and Marc Jacob’s Marc.

He has opened the shows for Hermes and Jill Stuart and closed for Gucci and Valentino.

His other prominent fashion campaigns include Prada, Hugo Boss, and Louis Vuitton.

10. Jon Kortajarena

The 36 years old Spanish model and actor ventured into the fashion world after being spotted by a booker at a fashion show in Barcelona while vacationing with his friend.

Since then, Jon Kortajarena has been a prominent face for Versace, Chanel, H & M, and Dolce & Gabbana.

GQ Mexico and Forbes ranked him “Model of the Year” and “one of the top male models” in 2009.

You can also have a glimpse of the model in Madonna‘s “Girl Gone Wild” and Kanye West‘s “Wolves” music video.

9. Hu Bing

Holding the ninth spot of the top male models in the world is none other than the Chinese fashion icon Hu Bing.

Just after a year of entering the fashion world in 1990, Hu Bing earned himself the honor of “Top Chinese Male Models” in 1991.

Besides, Hu Bing is also noted as the first Chinese male model to venture out with big international names in the fashion world, such as Louis Vuitton, Valentino, and Dolce & Gabbana.

You can also see his big name shining in the Chinese film industry with his best-known works in dramas like Love Talks (1999) and Pink Ladies (2003).

8. Sean O’ Pry

Forbe’s most successful model of all time, Sean O’ Pry, began his modeling venture at the age of 17.

The director of AIM Model Management, Nole Marin, eventually scouted O’ Pry when he saw his prom pictures on MySpace.

Soon the Georgian hunk went on to open up the runways for big names in the fashion industry like Versace, Saint Laurent, and Givenchy.

His face may be somehow familiar. Do you remember him playing the love interest of Taylor Swift in “Blank Space”?

7. David Gandy

Coming to this spot is one of the most eminent names in the men’s modeling world who revolutionized the game for male models with his spellbinding macho physique and chiseled features.

The British model David Gandy was Dolce & Gabbana’s muse who often led their runways and campaigns, most notably the “Light Blue” fragrance.

His other modeling creds include working for the likes of H&M, Zara, Hugo Boss, Marks & Spencer.

The glossy covers of British Vogue and GQ have always adorned his beauty.

While in his spare time, Gandy is an active blogger for British Vogue and British GQ.

6. Armando Cabral

The 38 years old black hunk from Guinea-Bissau, Armando Cabral, began his modeling venture in 2001 in Portugal.

Later, his breakthrough was in 2006, when New York’s IMG Model scouted him.

Since then, Cabral has graced the runways for Louis Vuitton, Dior Homme, and Thierry Mugler.

In 2014, Cabral started his luxury footwear line “Armando Cabral” in Lisbon.

5. Mark Vanderloo

Coming to the fifth spot is the international sensation in the men’s fashion world of the 90s and the 2000s.

The Dutch model Mark Vanderloo has nearly graced 50 runways for the Valentino, Gucci, Armani, and Hermes in his heydays.

The male supermodel, Vanderloo, is fluent in five different languages.

Further, the popular Mass Effect video game series has portrayed his face for its playable male character, Commander Shepard.

The VH1 even honored him as the “Model of the Year” back in 1996.

4. Lucky Blue Smith

A single glimpse of Lucky Blue Smith’s piercing blue eyes and platinum blonde hair, then you got it: a sudden heart-pounding and the whole zoo of butterflies fluttering inside your belly.

B.Smith has established himself in the modeling world since he was 12 years old.

Saint Laurent, Calvin Klein, and Tommy Hilfiger are among the brands he has modeled for.

When he is not modeling, you can find him drumming his kickass beats for his surf rock band, The Atomics.

3. Tyson Beckford

Ralph Lauren’s signature Polo model, Tyson Beckford, ventured into the fashion world when The Source Magazine’s editor luckily ran into him back in 1991.

The black supermodel has the same level of hype and success in the modeling industry as the 90s female supermodels.

Aside from modeling, Beckford has also pursued his career as an actor and host.

He appeared in the popular comedy film of 2001, Zoolander.

If you want to check him somewhere else, tune into DJ Khaled’s “Go Hard” and Mariah Carey’s “Infinity” music video.

2. Jordan Barrett

The luscious Australian hunk stormed into the modeling industry with IMG Australia at the tender age of 14.

Since then, he has graced the runways and campaigns for countless luxury lines from Tom Ford, Versace to Tommy Hilfiger with his euphoric charm.

GQ’s “Man of the Style” also earned himself the honor of Models.com “Model of the Year” in 2016.

In 2021, he exchanged the vows with late modeling agent John Casablancas’s son, Fernando Casablancas.

1. Alton Mason

At the top, we have a model from Nebraska.

Before storming into the modeling industry, Alton Mason was spotted as the backup dancer in Diddy’s 2015 BET Awards.

The model is Chanel’s first-ever colored model to grace its runway with his allure.

His other runaway creds include bombing the shows of Gucci, Tom Ford, Versace, and Louis Vuitton.

Soon, the world’s top male model, Mason, is about to star in the musical biopic Elvis as “Little Richard” under the direction of Baz Luhrman.
