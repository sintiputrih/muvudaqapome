Brie Bella Bummed Rhea Ripley Isn't On WWE Main Roster

Rhea Ripley will likely be making her main roster debut soon. She was written out of NXT, and now comes the waiting game. As we all know, a debut can sometimes take a long time, and plans can always change.

While speaking on the Pretty Big Deal podcast, Brie Bella brought up Rhea Ripley while they were discussing great women on the WWE roster. Brie said that she’s “actually bummed” that WWE hasn’t called Rhea Ripley up to the main roster yet.

“Same with Rhea Ripley. I’m actually bummed she’s not on the main roster yet because she constantly puts out great matches. Her look is incredible, she’s presence going to the ring. She’s still in NXT, which is great for NXT, but she should have been on the main roster years ago. Especially her storyline with Charlotte. I was like, ‘That’s someone who should be headlining Raw.'”

There is no report about which brand Rhea Ripley is slated to debut on. Damian Priest was set to make his SmackDown debut, but Kevin Owens and Roman Reigns didn’t like the idea of inserting Priest as Owens’ best friend. He’s expected to be on the RAW brand now.

We’ll have to see what the future holds for Rhea Ripley, but Brie Bella will be watching.
