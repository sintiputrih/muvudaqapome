In addition to being an interior designer, Nathan Jay “Nate” Berkus (born September 17, 1971) is also an author, television presenter, and television personality. He is the owner of the Chicago-based interior design company Nate Berkus Associates, and he has been on The Oprah Winfrey Show on a regular basis, giving design advice to viewers and arranging surprise makeovers for people’s houses. A number of product lines have been launched by him, and he has written a number of books. In 2010, he launched his own television programme, The Nate Berkus Show, which included makeovers, culture, design, and personal counselling.
