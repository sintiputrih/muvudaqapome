Dylan Minnette-Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, In Relation, Facts

Table of Biography

Dylan Minnette is a prominent young American actor. Dylan Minnette is also a singer and musician involved with the band the Wallows. He is also a guitarist for this band. He is popular for films and TV shows like “13 Reasons Why“, “Lost”, “Awake”, “Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day”, etc.

Early Life and Childhood

Dylan Minnette was born on December 29, 1996, and he is celebrating his 27th birthday now. His full name is Dylan Christopher Minnette. Likewise, he hails from Evansville, Indiana, and his zodiac sign is Capricorn.

Talking about his family, his parents are his father Robyn, and his mother Craig Minnette. His family moved to Champaign, Illinois. From there, they moved to Los Angeles after living in Champaign for five years. His family moved to LA for him to pursue a career in acting.

Awards

Dylan Minnette received an award at the 2008 Young Artist Award. It was for the category “Best Performance in a TV Series – Young Actor Ten or Under” for his role as Clay Norman in the TV show Saving Grace.

At the 2011 Young Artist Award, he bagged the award for the category “Best Performance in a TV Series – Guest Starring Young Actor 14-17” for his role as Cameron in the TV show Medium.

Similarly, at the 2013 NBR Award, he took home the award “Best Ensemble” for the film Prisoners. 

He shared this award with his co-stars Hugh Jackman, Jake Gyllenhaal, Viola Davis, Maria Bello, Terrence Howard, Melissa Leo, Paul Dano, Zoë Soul, and Erin Gerasimovich. Apart from these, he has received numerous other nominations.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Dylan Minnette is a prominent actor, singer, and musician. He is popular for his role as “Clay Jensen” in the Netflix drama series 13 Reasons Why. He also starred in the drama series Lost and the fantasy series Awake. For the latter, he acted alongside Jason Isaacs.

He has also appeared in the drama series Scandal and Grey’s Anatomy. He also played the role of a young “Michael Scofield” in Prison Break.

Apart from being a prominent actor, he is also a musician and singer. He is also a guitarist and singer for the American alternative rock band Wallows.

Talking more about the films that he has starred in, he appeared in the horror film Let Me In released in 2010, and in the thriller film Prisoners released in 2013.

He also starred in the family comedy film Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day released in 2014 as well as the family fantasy film Goosebumps released in 2015.

The Beginning

An agent scouted Dylan Minnette in Chicago. Soon after that, he began to work on commercial modeling and acting at the young age of 7 years. The young actor scored his first major TV role at the young age of 8 years.

He played the role of young “Charlie Sheen” in the film Two and a Half Men released in 2003. Then in 2005, he appeared in an episode of the popular Nickelodeon show, Drake & Josh.

Among all his films and TV shows, he had his breakthrough role as “Clay Norman” in the TNT series Saving Grace. He later gained additional fame for his role as “Clay Jensen”. Clay is a character in the Netflix original series 13 Reasons Why.

In Music

Dylan Minnette is the singer and rhythm guitarist in the band Wallows. Other members of the band are drummer Cole Preston and singer/guitarist Braeden Lemasters. Before 2017, the band was famous as Feaver and Narwhals.

He and his band performed at “Summer Meltdown”. It is a concert for autism awareness and they performed at the event in the year 2010. They also won a battle of the bands contest the same year, sponsored by 98.7 FM.

The Wallows played at Vans Warped Tour 2011 and since then, they have performed at many Los Angeles venues including the Roxy Theatre and Whisky a Go-Go. Moreover, R.L. Stine’s The Haunting Hour used the band’s song Bleeding Man in the promo for its season 2.

Throughout 2017, the Wallows released four singles on Apple Music and Spotify. The singles are Pleaser, Sun Tan, Uncomfortable, and Pulling Leaves Off Trees.

Then in the year 2018, the band released their debut EP Spring. This includes the singles Pictures of Girls and These Days. He and his band also performed the first single on The Late Late Show with James Corden on 8 May 2018.

The band released their debut album called Nothing Happens in March 2019. In the year 2020, the band released their second EP called Remote which includes singles Nobody Gets Me (Like You) and Virtual Aerobics.
