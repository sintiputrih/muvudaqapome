Emily Wilson Job

According to LinkedIn, Emily Wilson started her career as an Intern at MassHousing – Rental Operations where she worked in 2015. Then, she landed her internship at CBRE Capital Markets around the same period. From September 2015 to May 2017, she worked as Co-Lead College Brand Ambassador –  Barbour for Cone Communications from September 2015 to May 2017.

Four months after quitting Cone Communications, she landed the job of Commerical Real Estate Event Producer at Bisnow where she worked until May 2018.

From June 2018 to January 2019, Situs hired Emily as a Business Development Analyst. Following that, she moved to UBS as an associate, in private wealth management where she worked until October 2021.

Currently based in Miami, Florida, she joined CrossTower as Director of Sales in October 2021. Starting from February 2022, she has been working as Director of Sales & Business Development.

Glassdoor.com reported that the Business development director in Miami makes $115,000 per year.

Coming to her academic qualifications, Emily completed her high school at Wellesley High School in 2013. She enrolled at Boston College where she studied Communications Management & Leadership. She also spent time in Italy as a transferred student and attended Florence University of the Arts where she spent five months.
