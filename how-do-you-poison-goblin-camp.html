How do you poison Goblin camp?

The easiest way to poison the Goblin Camp is to set one of the party members Invisible with a spell or potion. Auntie Ethel at the Druid Grove or Roah Moonglow at the Shattered Sanctum sells Invisibility potions for those without the spell. Once done, quickly head to the Booze Tub near X: -99 Y:442.

How do you poison the goblin camp in Baldur’s Gate?

A great way to kill some Goblins is by poisoning the Booze Tub. There is a tub in the courtyard, pictured above, that can be interacted with. A crafting screen will pop up where you will be able to add Basic Poison to the tub.

Is there a way to sneak out of the goblin camp?

Players can pick the lock on the jail cell and sneak out of the Worg Pens manually. Those who don’t have lockpicks or the proficiency to unlock the door will have to yank the dead elf off the mysterious hole in the other side of the cell and attack the hole until it breaks.
