About

Former political analyst for the radio show KIRO and Air America Radio. He is the son of former U.S. President Ronald Reagan.

Before Fame

He briefly attended Yale University, dropping out after one semester to pursue a career in ballet.

Trivia

He is known for his liberal views, a stark contrast to his father's Republican ways.

Family Life

He was born to Ronald and Nancy Reagan. He was married to Doria Palmieri from 1980 to 2014.

Associated With

He was a member of the board for the advocacy group Creative Coalition. Christopher Reeve and Alec Baldwin have also been active with the organization.
