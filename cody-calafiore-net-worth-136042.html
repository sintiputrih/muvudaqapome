Cody Calafiore Net Worth

Cody Calafiore is a reality TV star from Hackensack, New Jersey. He was born on December 13, 1990 and was a Seventeen Magazine "2014 Hot Guy Panelist." He is most well-known for being a house guest on the 16th season of CBS's hit reality series Big Brother.
