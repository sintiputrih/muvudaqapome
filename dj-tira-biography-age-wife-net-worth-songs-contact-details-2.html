DJ Tira Biography, Age, Wife, Net Worth, Songs &amp; Contact Details

DJ Tira (born 24 August 1976) whose real name is Mthokozi Khathi is a South African Disk Jockey, record producer, and a Durban kwaito artist.

He is the founder of a record label Afrotainment. He’s best known for introducing artists such as DJ C’ndo, Big Nuz, and DJ Fisherman through his record label Afrotainment.

DJ Tira is a graduate of the University of Natal where he studied Human Resources.

DJ Tira Age

He was born on the 24th of August 1976 in KwaHlabisa, a village located in the Northern parts of KwaZulu Natal. As of 2020, he is 44 years old.

Dj Tira Wife

Tira is married to Gugu Khathi. On October 2016, he and his wife Gugu Khathi hosted an Umabo ceremony at his home in KwaHlabisa, in KwaZulu-Natal.

The Umabo is a traditional Zulu ceremony where a bride gets formally introduced to her husband’s ancestors in a cow slaughtering ritual and celebration.

It normally takes place after the white wedding, and it is believed that one is not fully married until after the Umabo ceremony in Zulu culture.

DJ Tira Children

He has two sons with his wife who are Junior and Chase Khathi. Both have followed in their father’s musical footsteps.

Junior is dropping a hip-hop single soon and Chase loves spinning the decks.
