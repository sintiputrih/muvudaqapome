JJ Hannon to perform in Brazil this month

Jj Hannon is a singer, actor, viral social media sensation. He rose to fame initially by posting pictures of his everyday life on Instagram and videos singing on YouTube. By the age of only 16, he has been everywhere from movie sets, commercials, TV shows, to in live performances.

Hannon is set to be one first USA acts that isn’t a major artist to perform in Brazil and the shows are nearly sold out.

Hannon will perform in Rio de Janeiro on August 25th and then he will perform in São Paulo on September 2nd.
