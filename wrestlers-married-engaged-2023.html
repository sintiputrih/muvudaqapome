10 Wrestlers Who Got Married &amp; Engaged In 2023

2023 will indeed go down as one of the most surprising years in wrestling. A lot of unexpected events occurred – things that fans didn’t think would ever happen, such as WWE merging with Endeavor, or CM Punk finally returning to WWE.

Next year is likely going to be a blast for fans as well. Things will be different for many of the wrestlers too, as they won’t enter the new year alone. Lots of them got engaged this year, and some even got married as well. The following stars are some examples of that.

10 Buddy Mathews Got Rhea Ripley An Engagement Ring

They Work For Different Promotions

  • Buddy Matthews was previously engaged to Alexa Bliss
  • Buddy Matthews works for AEW
  • Rhea Ripley is the Women’s Champion

Rhea Ripley is one of the hottest acts in WWE today. A lot of fans already know that she is dating AEW wrestler, Buddy Matthews. The couple announced their engagement earlier this year in August via an Instagram post.

Last year, Ripley and Matthews had confirmed their relationship and their fans were very happy – and even jealous in Ripley’s case – after hearing this news.

9 Hulk Hogan Tied The Knot With Sky Daily

Revealed Their Relationship In 2022

  • 2 Time WWE Hall of Famer
  • Hogan was married two times before
  • The Hulkster has 2 children

Two years after his divorce, Hulk Hogan tied the knot once again. His current wife’s name is Sky Daily. They got married in September this year, as reported by TMZ. Just a few weeks before in July, they had announced their engagement. Sky is a yoga instructor by profession, and Hogan is now retired from wrestling.

8 Logan Paul Proposed To His Girlfriend Nina Agdal

Paul & Agdal Have Been Together For Over A Year

  • First met in New York City during an event
  • Logan Paul made his wrestling debut in 2022
  • Nina Agdal also dated Leonardo DiCaprio

Current WWE United States Champion Logan Paul also got engaged this year to his girlfriend Nina Agdal in July. They have been together since May 2022. She’s appeared on his YouTube channel many times, and Paul even shot a video of him proposing to Agdal and uploaded it on the video platform.

While he was planning the whole proposal in Italy, his fiancee found out about it and Logan tried to cover up for it by gifting her earrings instead.

7 Omos Walked Down The Aisle With Cheyenne Quailey

Have Been In A Relationship For Over A Decade

  • Omos is a former Raw Tag Team Champion in WWE
  • Omos’ wife is a doctor
  • Have been together for over a decade

While Omos didn’t achieve significant things this year in terms of wrestling, it was still a pretty big year for the former Raw Tag Team Champion as he married his girlfriend Cheyenne Quailey. Stars like Ricochet, Montez Ford, Bianca Belair, and more attended their wedding. Omos and Cheyenne started dating each other around 2012.

6 Samantha Irvin Agreed To Marry Ricochet

Samantha Works As An Announcer In WWE

  • Ricochet is a former United States and Intercontinental Champion
  • Working for WWE since 2018
  • Both have children from previous relationships

Ricochet and Samantha have been together since 2021. Fans already know that Samantha works as a ring announcer for WWE. Their relationship was even used as part of Ricochet’s rivalry with Logan Paul earlier this year. They confirmed their engagement in January 2023.

5 Jinny Said “I Do” To Gunther

Have Been Together Since 2019

  • Jinny was released by WWE in 2023
  • She retired from wrestling due to a concussion
  • Confirmed their relationship through Instagram

There’s no doubt that Gunther is having a great year. In September he became the longest reigning Intercontinental Champion in WWE history and earlier in May, he married former NXT wrestler Jinny. They confirmed their relationship back in November 2019 and before that, Gunther was already married.

4 Emma Said “Yes” To Riddick Moss

Confirmed Their Relationship In August 2022

  • Emma returned to WWE last year
  • Riddick Moss and Emma teamed up on SmackDown
  • The couple is in their 30s

2023 brought both ups and downs for Emma and Riddick Moss. They did get engaged in June, but shortly afterward both of them were released from their WWE contracts in September. Riddick seemed like he was getting a push, but after Triple H took over things took a turn for him. Emma had also returned last year in WWE and was aligned with Moss on-screen.

3 Solo Sikoa Exchanged Vows With Almia Williams

Have Two Children Together

Solo Sikoa married his girlfriend, Almia Williams, in February. They already have two children from their relationship, Za’khi Christopher and Zion. Solo and Almia are believed to have started dating when they were at Dickinson State University.

2 Dominik Mysterio Got Engaged To Marie Juliette

Dating Since Childhood

  • Dominik Mysterio is one of the best heels today
  • On-screen he's in a relationship with Rhea Ripley
  • Has been with his fiancee since high school

While Dominik Mysterio and Rhea Ripley are portrayed as a couple on WWE television, both wrestlers have different partners in real life. Fans already know that Ripley is in a relationship with Buddy Matthews, but they don’t know too much about Dom’s romantic life. He’s engaged to Marie Juliette, who’s his high-school sweetheart. They’ve been together for over a decade now.

1 Sonya Deville Asked Her Girlfriend Toni Cassano To Marry Her

Dating Since 2019

  • Sonya started liking Cassano when she was in a relationship
  • Cassano sent Deville a DM after her breakup and they started talking
  • Deville is currently inactive in WWE

The reason why Sonya Deville isn’t appearing in WWE right now is because she’s injured. Deville suffered an ACL injury back in July and had to leave in the middle of her Women’s Tag Team Championship reign. Most fans already know that Sonya is gay, and she’s always been open about this. Deville dated several people before coming into a relationship with fitness model Toni Cassano in 2019. They got engaged earlier this year in February. Sonya popped the question after planning the proposal for four months.
