How did Liza Koshy get famous?

published on May 17, 2024
American actress, Liza Koshy became famous in 2013 when she started posting funny videos on the then-popular social media platform, Vine, under the pseudonym “Lizza.” She usually posted short comic videos. In 2017, when Vine closed down, she has over 7 million followers. She took her talent to YouTube to continue entertaining her fans in 2016. Currently, she has managed to gather 17 million subscribers and two YouTube channels and has a total of over 3 billion views. Lois Lowry Biography Lois Lowry is an American writer and author who was born and brought up in Honolulu, Hawaii, United States of America. She is widely known for authoring several books for children and young adults, including Number the Stars and The Giver Quartet. Lowry is also recognized for writing about difficult subject matters and complex themes in works for young audiences. In 1990, she won two Newbery Medals: for Number the Stars and The Giver 1994.
