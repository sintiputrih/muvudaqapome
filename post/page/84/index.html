New Jersey father Dennis Vassallo started a Facebook group dedicated to sharing stories about random acts of kindness after his son Dylan Vassallo’s suicide in 2015. The Kindness Challenge, which Dennis initially started for friends and family on January 31, asks people to perform a daily act of kindness, whether it’s holding the door or giving someone a compliment.
