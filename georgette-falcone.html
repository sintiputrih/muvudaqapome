Who is Georgette Falcone

Georgette Falcone is an American child actress who is the younger daughter of actor, comedian, artist, producer, and fashion designer Melissa McCarthy and actress, comedian, and filmmaker Ben Falcone. Dubbed as a “force of nature” by her mum, Georgette grows upright under the spotlight. In spite of this, her parents are trying to give both her and her sibling a semblance of normality in their personal lives. She made her acting debut in her mother’s movie ‘The Manager.’ After hearing that her older sister, Vivian, was going to act, she decided to do the same thing. Her parents acquiesced and she made a comedy debut in 2016. According to her father, neither she nor her sister has made yet another offer for employment.
