Toke Makinwa Biography

Toke Makinwa was born on 3rd November 1984 in Lagos State in the south western part of Nigeria. She is currently 36 years old. She grew up in Lagos where she had her Primary school education and obtains her First School Leaving Certificate.

She completed her secondary education at the Federal Government Girls’ College in Oyo State, where she obtained her West African Senior School Certificate, she then proceeded to the University of Lagos where she graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in English and Literature.
