Who Is Cheer's Kailee Peppers? Her Bio, Age, Boyfriend, Height, Career

Featured on the 2nd installment of Cheer, Kailee Peppers’ coaching journey was full of troubles. This former Navarro cheerleader returned to the team as their new assistant coach, and right at the start, she’s got herself into some beef with her former teammate/then student La’Darius Marshall. 

Keep reading this Kailee Peppers Bio to learn more about her.

Kailee Peppers On Netflix’s Cheer Season 2

Kailee Peppers was invited to Navarro as their new assistant coach because the head coach Monica Aldama had to leave the team to compete on Dancing With the Stars in 2020.

So, as the new assistant coach Kailee and Andy Cosferent started the season well. Unfortunately, not long after Kailee and the fan-favorite La’Darius Marshall got into a drama over a dog. 

Reportedly, La’Darius adopted two dogs and gave one to Kailee to keep watch until the end of the semester. But Kailee thought the dog was hers to keep, which resulted in a scuffle! Sadly, this also ultimately led to La’Darius abruptly leaving the show partway through the season.

According to La’Darius, he wasn’t getting the support he needed from the coaching staff while Monica was gone. And apparently, it didn’t get any better after the head coach returned as well.

As for Kailee, she didn’t respond to the matter. However, she did talk about her time on Cheer and mentioned she had “so much fun being a part of this season with all of my favorite people and the amazing film crew.”

Kailee Peppers Age

Kailee Peppers was born on July 2, 1996. That made her 25 years of age when she first appeared on Cheer in 2022.

Corresponding to her birthday, Kailee is of the Cancer zodiac.

Does Kailee Peppers Have A Boyfriend?

Kailee Peppers was once rumored to be dating the Navarro cheerleader Matthew David. But the star herself never confirmed that he was her boyfriend. The two appeared to be together since 2016, and the last we saw them over Kailee IG was in April 2019 post captioned, “One last time with my people.”

Also, back in 2016, when the two were supposedly in love. Kailee did make some lovey-dovey tweets such as — “Just wish I could see my boyfriend every day; or I’m obsessed with my boyfriend.”

Then fast forward to Feb 2019, Kailee went on to reveal that her boyfriend was the first person to ever not tip her. “He said, ‘you didn’t introduce yourself,’” she recalled.

As for Kailee’s dating life before 2016, it didn’t seem like she was in any serious relationship before that. “I just want a cute tall boyfriend who’s not annoying is that too much to ask for,” she once tweeted in 2013.

Fun Fact: Kailee once believed that she’ll never have a boyfriend.

Kailee Peppers Height

Kailee Peppers stands tall at a height of 5 feet 5 inches (165 cm).

Mentioning her distinct features, she has hazel eyes, dark straight hair, and a triangular face.

Kailee Peppers Career

Kailee Peppers was a cheerleader for as long as she can remember.

Having cheered since she was a little girl, Kailee was quick to realize that she wanted to pursue a career in the industry. So, she enrolled herself at Navarro College and moved to Corsicana, Texas. There, she was coached by the renowned Coach Monica Aldama for three years, during which she also competed for the team during their 2015 to 2017 seasons.

In her third year, Kailee was even offered the position of Navarro’s first-ever Assistant Cheer Coach for the 2017-18 season. But she wasn’t just ready to be on the sidelines yet. Thus, she went on to cheer for Texas Tech University for the next two years and finally returned to coaching, alongside Andy Cosferent in Cheer season 2.

So, what is she doing now? Well after Cheer came to an end, Kailee left Navarro once again and joined the Texas Tech University cheerleading squad, also going back to the co-ed program for her third year.

Despite being a coach, she’s still practicing hard because cheer is much more than a passion to her. We have no idea what’s her next plan, but one thing for sure — We’ll be seeing more of her on the TV.
