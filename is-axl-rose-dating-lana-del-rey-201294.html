Is Axl Rose Dating Lana Del Rey?

This rumored new couple has fans scratching their heads! 

Axl Rose, 50, was photographed leaving Chateau Marmont in West Hollywood with Lana Del Rey, 25, on Friday.

The twosome then took off together in the same SUV later that night.

Indeed, the "Video Games" singer has been a longtime fan of the Guns N' Roses rocker.

Before she made it big, Del Rey (real name: Elizabeth Grant) recorded a song dedicated to Rose titled, "Axl Rose Husband." In the song she sings, "You're my one king daddy, I'm your little queen."

Last month, Del Rey was spotted attending two back-to-back Guns N' Roses concerts in L.A.. An eyewitness at the March 12 concert in Hollywood told US Weekly that the moody new star "didn't take her eyes off the stage. She looked like such a fan. She was in total awe of the guys on stage."
